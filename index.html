OUR PILLARS OF DEVELOPMENT

Designed by community leaders, the Heart Hustle is more than a standard race series. We balance athletic skill improvement with hydration awareness and cardiac wellness templates.

01

Athletic Skill Progression

Developing running form, endurance, and pacing logic over six structured sessions. Perfect for both absolute beginners and competitive youngsters.

Cardiovascular Focus • Core Endurance
02

Youth Hydration & Safety

Every participant learns the science of proper electrolyte intake, warming up securely, and listening to their heart rates during extreme movement cycles.

Hydration Gear Ordered • Safe Running Routes
03

Mentorship and Leadership

Mentored actively by dedicated high school & collegiate athletes of the Green Brook Leo Club. Developing leadership pathways that bridge generation lines.

Lions International • Community Service

Frequently Asked Questions

Our program is intentionally optimized for children aged **5 to 13**. The weekly coaching runs are split into three tiered groups based on age and basic running endurance to keep programs fair, exciting, and highly secure.
Safety is our number one priority. Our running pathways in Arthur Lewis Park are fully cordoned off from vehicles. We have designated spotters every 100 meters, mandatory high-visibility safety jerseys for children, dynamic pacing guides, and active checking stations.
No, the **Heart Hustle Series** is completely free! The program is entirely sponsored and coordinated by the **Green Brook Leo Club** as part of our youth leadership initiatives.
In the event of heavy rain or hazardous heat waves, we send out an SMS and email notification exactly 2 hours prior to start time. Weather make-ups will automatically roll to scheduled slots during the following weekend.
